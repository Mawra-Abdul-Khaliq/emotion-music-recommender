Music Recommendation System Based On
User's Facial Emotions

The purpose of this music recommendation web-app is to enhance user's emotional well-being by recommending songs based on their facial expressions. By analyzing real-time facial emotions, the system identifies the user's current mood and suggests music that resonates with or helps transition their emotional state. It aims to provide a personalized music experience that can uplift, calm, or energize users as needed. The web-app uses advanced technology to detect emotions and has a large database of music details. Machine learning helps make accurate recommendations and learns from user feedback. The goal is to offer a personalized, engaging, and emotionally supportive music experience.
